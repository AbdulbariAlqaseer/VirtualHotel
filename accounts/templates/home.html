About Mariotte

Enjoy an unforgettable experience at the Mariotte RJ Copacabana, located in one of the most spectacular places in Rio de Janeiro, the Copacabana Beach, under the majestic gaze of Sugar Loaf Mountain. Inspired by the glamour carioca in the 1950's, this luxury hotel is an oasis. There are 10 rooms categories with a balcony, and the Gold Rooms offer a private lounge and exclusive services. The hotel also has two swimming pools, spa, sauna, fitness center, restaurant, bars and meeting rooms.

Accommodation

our 375 apartments, with 53 suites, make the fairmont gold floor. They are decorated with tones of blue, inspired in the sea of Copacabana. All the apartments offer generous comfort supplemented by the stunning views of Copacabana beach, the Sugar Loaf or the city.
